App Features

Mcdonald's eLearning takes the paper forms, called Station Observation Checklists (SOCs), that McDonalds currently use and turn them into a eLearning application with additional features

Digital SOCs

Digital Station Observation Checklists that offer the user a gamified experience to learning

Review SOCs

Ability to review Station Observation Checklist results and give feedback to the employee

Review Progression

Ability to review employee progression and identify their weak areas

Leaderboards

Indiviual Station Observation Checklist leaderboards where employees scores are displayed encouraging them to improve

Modify and Edit

Ability to modify and edit Station Observation Checklists as the business evolves

Technolgoies

The following technologies were used in the development of McDonalds eLearning:

  • Ionic Framework
  • Angular
  • JavaScript
  • Firebase
